## Objectives
- Provide healthcare professionals with AI-driven summarization and decision-support tools.
- Reduce time spent on documentation and research navigation.
- Improve patient education and care navigation through accessible AI interfaces.

## Target Users
- Doctors and nurses needing quick summaries of patient records.
- Researchers requiring literature reviews and compliance documentation.
- Patients seeking understandable explanations of medical information.

## Key Features
- Clinical document summarization (patient records, lab reports).
- Research paper summarization and compliance assistance.
- Patient-facing chatbot for education and navigation.
- Multilingual support (English + Indian languages).

## Constraints
- Use only synthetic or publicly available datasets.
- Ensure transparency and explainability of AI outputs.
- Avoid providing medical diagnoses; tool is for support, not replacement.

## Success Metrics
- Reduction in time spent on documentation by healthcare professionals.
- Accuracy and clarity of AI-generated summaries.
- Patient satisfaction and improved understanding of medical information.
